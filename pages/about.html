About ImgKitLab

ImgKitLab is a collection of free online image tools that run entirely in your web browser. We built this site for people who need quick image compression, resizing, format conversion, or metadata removal — without installing software or uploading files to a cloud server.

How it works

Every tool on ImgKitLab uses the browser Canvas API to process images locally on your device. When you drop an image into a tool, it is read into memory, processed by JavaScript, and offered back to you as a download. Your images are never sent to our servers — we do not operate image-processing backends.

Why browser-based?

  • Privacy — Sensitive photos and work files stay on your computer.
  • Speed — No upload wait time for large files.
  • Accessibility — Works on any modern desktop or mobile browser without installs.

Who is this for?

ImgKitLab is used by photographers, web developers, social media managers, students, and anyone who needs a fast, private way to prepare images for the web, email, or print.
