Frequently Asked Questions

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. Compression uses the Canvas API in your browser. Your file never leaves your device.

What quality setting should I use?

80% is a good balance for web use. Lower values mean smaller files but more visible compression artifacts.

Can I compress PNG files too?

Yes. PNG files will be exported as JPEG. Use our PNG compressor for PNG-specific options.
