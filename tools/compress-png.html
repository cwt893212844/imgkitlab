Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my PNG still large after compression?

PNG is lossless. For photos, converting to JPEG or WebP usually produces much smaller files. Try our PNG to WebP converter.

Will transparency be preserved?

Yes with PNG and WebP output. JPEG does not support transparency — transparent areas become white.

Are images uploaded?

No. Everything runs in your browser using the Canvas API.
