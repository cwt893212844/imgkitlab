Frequently Asked Questions

Why convert PNG to WebP?

WebP typically produces files 25–35% smaller than PNG with similar visual quality, ideal for websites.

Is WebP lossless?

This tool uses lossy WebP at your chosen quality. For photos, quality 80–90 is usually excellent.

Does my browser support WebP export?

All modern browsers support WebP. If conversion fails, try Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.
